Matt was born in Australia in 1976 and raised in a small town. Since finishing school he has studied journalism, maritime logistics and Japanese and has worked in a variety of industries in both blue and white collar roles in three different countries.

For two years Matt lived in England, working in pubs and nursing homes. He also lived in Japan for four years working at Nova as well as elementary schools junior high schools. Six months after resigning Nova went bankrupt, Matt is almost certain the collapse of the Eikaiwa giant was nothing to do with him.

Matt currently works as an administrator for an industrial waste management company in the regional city of Gladstone in Queensland Australia. While he has no prior work experience in software or coding he is interested in developing his programming skills

Among other activities, he has been known to participate in Mikoshi festivals, climb the occasional mountain if asked and indulge in his interest of reading and history.
